How it works

01

Tap to record

Single press on the haptic button. LED pulses amber. You're capturing.

02

Audio buffered

Recorded on-device with noise cancellation. Syncs to your phone or directly to cloud via LTE-M.

03

AI transcribes

Cloud-side speech-to-text with speaker diarisation. Transcript ready in seconds.

04

Context flows

Transcripts push to your AI apps automatically. Ask Claude about your meeting. Search your day in Notion.
